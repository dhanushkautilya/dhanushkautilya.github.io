Who I Am

Hi, I’m Thirumala Dhanush Kammaripalle — a lifelong tech enthusiast and someone who’s always been curious about how technology shapes the world around us. Ever since I was a kid, I found myself drawn to computers, breaking things down to understand how they work and trying to build my own ideas into reality. This passion led me to pursue a Bachelor’s in Computer Science from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University and later a Master’s in Computer Science at George Mason University. During these years, I explored various domains within tech, but what stuck with me the most was the ability to use software to solve real-world problems and make things better — faster, smarter, and easier. Professionally, I’ve spent over 3+ years working as a Salesforce Certified Developer, building and optimizing enterprise-level CRM solutions. My work spans Apex, LWC, Visualforce, and integrations across Sales, Service, and Community Clouds. I take pride in crafting solutions that not only meet business goals but also improve user experience and system scalability.

Beyond Salesforce, I’m genuinely passionate about Artificial Intelligence and the incredible potential of Generative AI in transforming industries. I spend time learning about how these technologies can be applied in CRM, automation, personalization, and everyday tools we use. Outside of work, I’m a big fan of sports. I love playing and watching basketball, badminton, and cricket. Whether it’s shooting hoops with friends or catching a live match, sports keep me grounded, energized, and inspired. They’ve taught me teamwork, discipline, and how to thrive under pressure — qualities that also fuel my professional life.
